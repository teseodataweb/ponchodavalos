Why Invest in Marina Vallarta?

Marina Vallarta offers Puerto Vallarta's most complete luxury lifestyle with an 18-hole golf course, full-service marina, and gated communities just minutes from the international airport. With properties from $400,000 USD, 7.9% annual appreciation, and a 166% ROI, it is the top choice for families and investors seeking premier residential living.

Area Overview

Marina Vallarta at a Glance

Marina Vallarta represents the pinnacle of planned residential living in Puerto Vallarta, a master-planned community that seamlessly integrates luxury living with world-class recreational amenities. The centerpiece is an 18-hole golf course that winds through the community, complemented by a full-service marina accommodating vessels up to 200 feet, creating a lifestyle that appeals to the most discerning international buyers. Gated subdivisions within Marina Vallarta provide 24-hour security, manicured landscapes, and a genuine sense of community that has attracted families, retirees, and professionals from across North America. The neighborhood's proximity to Gustavo Díaz Ordaz International Airport makes it exceptionally convenient for frequent travelers and those maintaining homes in multiple countries. Real estate in Marina Vallarta spans from contemporary condominiums with golf course views starting at $400,000 USD to waterfront penthouses and estates exceeding $1.2 million. Current developments including Velōa Residences with 94 units, Marina Towers with 50 premium units, Shangri-La with 28 exclusive residences, and Alisia with just 12 ultra-luxury units showcase the breadth of options available. The 7.9% annual appreciation rate and 166% ROI demonstrate the sustained strength of this market segment. Poncho Dávalos brings AMPI-certified expertise and deep local connections to help international buyers navigate every aspect of purchasing in Marina Vallarta.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a property cost in Marina Vallarta?

Properties in Marina Vallarta range from $400,000 to $1,200,000 USD, with prices per square meter between $3,000 and $6,000 USD depending on golf course, marina, or ocean views.

Is Marina Vallarta good for families?

Absolutely. Marina Vallarta's gated communities with 24/7 security, golf course, international schools, and quiet residential atmosphere make it Puerto Vallarta's premier family-friendly neighborhood.

What new developments are available in Marina Vallarta?

Current projects include Velōa Residences (94 units from $400k), Marina Towers (50 units from $600k), Shangri-La (28 units from $500k), and the ultra-exclusive Alisia (12 units from $700k), with deliveries from 2026 to 2028.

What is the appreciation rate in Marina Vallarta?

Marina Vallarta records an average appreciation of 7.9% per year, among the highest in Puerto Vallarta. The master-planned community with premium amenities ensures consistent long-term property value growth.
