Area Overview

Zona Romántica at a Glance

Zona Romántica stands as the cultural and culinary epicenter of Puerto Vallarta, a neighborhood that perfectly encapsulates the magic of old Mexico while embracing a modern, cosmopolitan identity. Walking through its cobblestone streets lined with bougainvillea, you encounter art galleries, farm-to-table restaurants, and boutique shops that reflect the creative energy of its diverse community. The Malecón boardwalk and Playa de los Muertos anchor the neighborhood's appeal, drawing visitors and potential residents from across North America and Europe who fall in love with its authentic charm. As a real estate investment, Zona Romántica delivers some of Puerto Vallarta's strongest returns, with vacation rental occupancy rates consistently above 75% year-round. The area's established reputation as the most walkable and vibrant neighborhood in the city ensures sustained demand from short-term renters, digital nomads seeking monthly stays, and snowbirds looking for their winter escape. Current developments including SOHO PV with 124 luxury units, Praia Resort with 45 units, and the boutique Finca Los Agaves project with 8 exclusive units offer entry points from $450,000 to over $1 million USD. The 166% ROI and 7.9% annual appreciation reflect a market that rewards investors with both cash flow and equity growth. How much does a condo cost in Zona Romántica?

Condo prices in Zona Romántica range from $250,000 to $600,000 USD, with a price per square meter between $2,500 and $5,000 USD depending on beach proximity and building amenities.

Is vacation rental profitable in Zona Romántica?

Yes, Zona Romántica is one of Puerto Vallarta's most profitable vacation rental zones with a 166% average ROI and occupancy rates above 75% annually on platforms like Airbnb and Booking, driven by steady tourist and digital nomad demand.

What new developments are there in Zona Romántica?
